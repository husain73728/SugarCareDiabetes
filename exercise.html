Recommended Workouts

Scientifically backed routines for glucose stability.

Brisk Walking

schedule 30 mins daily

Improves blood sugar control and cardiovascular endurance through steady aerobic activity.

Yoga

schedule 20 mins

Reduces stress & improves metabolism by balancing the endocrine system and calming the mind.

Cycling

schedule 25–40 mins

Enhances cardiovascular health while being low-impact on joints, perfect for daily movement.

Light Strength Training

schedule 15–20 mins

Builds muscle & improves insulin use by increasing the body's metabolic demand for glucose.

Jogging

schedule 20 mins

Burns calories & stabilizes glucose by engaging large muscle groups in rhythmic motion.

Stretching

schedule 10 mins

Improves flexibility & circulation, essential for preventing injury and maintaining flow.

Exercise Tips for Diabetes Patients

  • slow_motion_video

    Start slow

    Begin with low-intensity sessions and gradually increase duration as your fitness improves.

  • water_drop

    Stay hydrated

    Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout to maintain fluid balance.

  • monitoring

    Monitor blood sugar

    Check levels before and after exercise to understand how your body responds to activity.
